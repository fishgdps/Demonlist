Rules

    5. ^ Uploads must be sent through YouTube to count as proof. Unlisted videos are allowed aslong as "Embed".
  5. Levels cannot be buffed or altered gameplay wise after being added to the list.
  6. Any record submitted before a new rule was added does not have the rule apply to it unless stated otherwise.
